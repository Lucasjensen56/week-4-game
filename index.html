Potion Counter

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four Potions below. By clicking on a Potion you will add a specific amount of points to your total score

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your score goes above the random number

The value of each Potion is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Potion
